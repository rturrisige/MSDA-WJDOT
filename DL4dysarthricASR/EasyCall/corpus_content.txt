EasyCall V1 contains speech recordings from 13 dysarthric speakers and 11 healthy speakers, that are
f01, f02, f03, f04, m01, m02, m03, m04, m05, m06, m07, m08, m09;
fc01, fc02, fc03, fc04, mc01, mc02, mc03, mc04, mc05, mc06, mc07.

Each speaker folder contain many subfolders corresponding to the recording sessions.

In the second version, the command "Esci da rubrica" has been added. Further, for each session the key command "Apri rubrica" is recorded three times instead of once. 
The EasyCall V2 includes 
- 12 dysarthric speakers: f05, f05, f07, f08, f09, m11, m12, m13, m14, m15, m16, m17;
- 12 healthy speakers: fc05, fc06, fc07, fc08, fc09, fc10, mc08, mc09, mc10, mc11, mc12, mc13.
